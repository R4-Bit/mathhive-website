Why I started MathHive

I started MathHive Tutoring because I believe math should be fun, exciting, and something every student can be proud of. My mission is to push students 2–3 grade levels above where they currently are — fast-tracking them toward higher learning and a lifetime of confidence. MathHive is a place where students discover their strengths, find real-world applications for math, and learn to show off what they know.
